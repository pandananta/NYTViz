Can You Guess When These NYT Articles Were Written Based on Their Headlines?


We analyzed 3,000+ New York Times titles to see how they evolved over time. This is what we found.

Title length (average number of words) is increasing.

The Gunning Fog Index (years of education required for comprehension) of titles in decreasing.

Title length (average number of syllables) is constant.

Title complexity (ratio of meaning to grammatical unit) is decreasing.
